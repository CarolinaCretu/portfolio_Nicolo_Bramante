Editor

Since November 2024, I've worked with the LFL, shooting and editing a variety of content. This includes creating gym interview videos for ELEVATES featuring the fighters, producing high-energy bumpers to hype up the crowd right before the fights, and crafting videos for the venue screen. I also had the opportunity to create aftermovies for the amateur fights at LFL 16. This experience has allowed me to blend my passion for film production with live event coverage, while sharpening my skills in fast-paced, high-stakes environments. It's also been a perfect opportunity to combine my love for mixed martial arts with my work in media.
