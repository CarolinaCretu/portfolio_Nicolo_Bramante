Acting

After completing an 8-week Acting for Film workshop at NYFA in 2019 at 18 years of age, I began my professional acting journey in 2021. I landed two bit parts and a supporting role in family films. While much of my work has been in this genre, I also see myself fitting into romantic films, dramas, and action movies.
Dakota

My first professional acting experience, Dakota is a family film starring Abbie Cornish, Billy Baldwin, and Megan Fox. I played Brad, a clumsy firefighter in training alongside Kate (Abbie Cornish). Directed by Kirk Harris, this role was a great introduction to working on set with seasoned actors.

More about the movie Dakota: IMDb

Vote for Santa / La Fabbrica del Natale

My second professional acting role, Vote for Santa started as a bit part but grew into a supporting role. I played Greg, a nerdy elf with a crush on the main character, Ella (Natalie Shinnick). Directed by the late Francesco Cinquemani, the film starred Lance Henriksen, Leonardo Cecchi and Natasha Henstridge.

More about the movie Vote for Santa: IMDb

A Day with Santa / Natale con i Puffins

In A Day with Santa, I reprised my role as Greg in the same Santa universe, now set hundreds of years later. Chaos erupts in Santa's factory when a wild visitor from Earth (Billy Baldwin) arrives. Also directed by Francesco Cinquemani (RIP), this film gave me another fun opportunity to step into the world of Christmas magic.
