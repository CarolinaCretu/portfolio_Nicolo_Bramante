Adrenaline is an action-packed crime drama series I've been developing since June 2023. Set in present-day New York City, the story follows a brave young NYC native, Lil J (Aurio Vida), who risks it all to take down the cartel when they threaten to destroy everything he's worked for. I wrote and produced the pilot and wrote two seasons. Directed and co-produced by Caio Lourenço De Melo, Adrenaline stars Aurio Vida, Jamie Gene, Joshua Albano, and Emiljano Lamushi in key roles. With gripping action and a raw, real-world storyline, Adrenaline brings high stakes to every moment.
